2. GENERAL TERMS & CONDITIONS OF SALE OF ACTIVITIES


2.1. BOOKING


The reservation of an Activity implies that you have already reserved your cottage and can only take place during your stay. You will have the possibility, once connected with your login, of reserving one or more Activities.


How to reserve an Activity during your stay?


Once on the presentation screen for Activities:


  1. Choose the method of consultation for the Activities

  2. Click on the desired activity

  3. Complete the required information

  4. Add to basket

  5. Type in your login (the cottage number followed by the first three letters of your family name), or scan the QR code that was included in your introduction package.

  6. Select “Continue my reservations” or “Go to checkout”

  7. To definitively confirm the order, repeat step 5 if requested, then tick the box for acceptance of the GTCU.

  8. Once the basket is validated, proceed to payment by bank card on the kiosk’s payment device.

  9. Add your payment details

  10. Confirm payment

  11. Once the payment is authorised, this is confirmed by a pop-up and a voucher is printed off that allows you to access the Activity. The payment receipt, to be kept, will also be printed off.

The reservation of an Activity is possible up until 15 minutes before the beginning of the Activity, which is shown on the notice describing the details of the Activity.


For safety reasons, some of the Activities are subject to strict eligibility conditions. These conditions are displayed on the booking terminal screen before you finally confirm your booking. If a customer confirms his/her booking without checking these conditions beforehand, he/she may subsequently be refused entry to the Activity.


Any reservation of an Activity for the participation of an underage child will be subject to parental authorisation, to be collected, completed and returned signed to reception at the resort. In the absence of this authorisation the underage child may be refused access to the Activity.


2.2. PAYMENT


Payment for the Activity should be made at the time of reservation on the Kiosk.


Payment methods: Only by bank card, the cards accepted are Visa, Master Card, Maestro and Amex.


2.3. RESERVATION CONFIRMATION

After authorisation and payment of the Activity, the Kiosk produces a voucher, that the client will also find in digital format in the “My tickets” section of the Planet Center Parcs app if the version is equipped with a MyCP account and the application.


The ticket must be presented in order to participate in the activity.
